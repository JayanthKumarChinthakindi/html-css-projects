Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is the integration process?

ScaleUp works with a simple npm package or an embedded script hook. Once you drop our client library in, telemetry event collection starts instantly.

Are there any limits on Starter or Pro tiers?

Starter provides basic event telemetry with community-only access. Pro adds automated system scaling limits of up to 5 instances, while Enterprise unlocks unlimited throughput and dedicated VPC channels.

Is there support for customized dashboard views?

Yes! You can completely build, drag-and-drop, and filter custom panel parameters on our Pro and Enterprise dashboards to fit your operations perfectly.
