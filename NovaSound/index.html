FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about NovaSound? Find quick answers below. If you need more help, feel free to contact us.

How does Hybrid Active Noise Cancellation work?

Our Hybrid ANC uses both external (feedforward) and internal (feedback) microphones to capture external ambient sound and sound inside the ear cup. It then generates an inverse sound wave to cancel both out, creating an ultra-quiet listening zone.

Can I connect NovaSound to multiple devices at once?

Yes! NovaSound features Bluetooth 5.2 Multipoint technology, allowing you to connect to two devices simultaneously (for example, your laptop and phone). It will seamlessly transition audio based on which device is active.

How long does the battery last, and does it support fast charge?

The battery lasts up to 50 hours with Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) turned off, and 40 hours with ANC active. With fast-charging technology, you can get 5 hours of play time from just a 10-minute USB-C charging session.

What is included in the packaging box?

Every NovaSound package includes the headphones, a premium hard-shell carrying case, a 1-meter USB-C charging cable, a 3.5mm auxiliary audio cable for wired listening, and a quick-start manual guide.

What does the 2-year warranty cover?

Our 2-year manufacturer warranty covers any manufacturing defects, hardware failures (battery, drivers, button sensors), and materials issues under normal consumer use. It does not cover accidental damage or wear-and-tear of pads.
