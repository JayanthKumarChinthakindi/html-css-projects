Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is there a free trial?
Yes, our Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial. You can configure APIs, process mock sandbox payments, and test instant payout features before your card is charged.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. FlowPay features month-to-month contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription directly through the dashboard billing settings at any time without penalty.
Is my data secure?
FlowPay utilizes enterprise-grade security. We are PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant, which is the gold standard for global payment security. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 protocols.
Do you offer enterprise support?
Yes! Our Enterprise clients receive dedicated account engineers, custom routing integrations, priority support queues, and signed Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with 99.99% uptime guarantees.
What payment methods are accepted?
FlowPay accepts all major credit/debit card issuers (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), global bank transfers (ACH, SEPA), and localized methods like iDEAL and Pix.
